# Trismegistus Continuity Research Loop Spine

Created: 2026-06-30

## The Point

Trismegistus should not be presented as a chatbot.

The public-safe frame is:

> Trismegistus is a continuity architecture for an AI expert partner: it keeps
> track of its own work, preserves receipts, compares baseline behavior against
> architecture-on behavior, and turns each result into the next research gate.

Chat, Telegram, browser missions, SWE-bench repair, WebArena traces, outreach,
and paid-work lanes are interfaces into the same continuity loop.

## What Makes It Different

Most demos show a surface.

Trismegistus is designed to show the loop under the surface:

1. remember the mission and prior receipts
2. gather source-backed evidence
3. compare baseline versus architecture-on behavior
4. take bounded action
5. save SQL / JSON / Markdown receipts
6. inspect the failure
7. patch the route
8. run the next gate

That is why the work matters for a research role. The claim is not that one
chat response is special. The claim is that the system is being built to carry
research continuity across time, tools, benchmarks, and operator channels.

## Application Translation

For Nous, the clean translation is:

> I am applying with a living research artifact. Trismegistus is a
> Hermes-aligned AI expert partner that turns hard research targets into a
> source map, baseline, architecture-on route, eval harness, worker loop, and
> receipt trail.

This is the same kind of work a forward deployed or research engineer has to
do in the field: enter a complex environment, define what evidence would count,
build the loop, test it, and improve it without losing the thread.

## AGI / ASI Boundary

Use:

> This is a road-toward-advanced-recursive-AI research build.

Use:

> It is a generative, self-referential research loop: the system preserves what
> it did, studies the gap, and asks what needs to improve next.

Do not use:

> AGI achieved.

Do not use:

> ASI achieved.

The public claim is process and receipts, not final consciousness language.

## Core Sentence For The Final Commercial

Trismegistus is where the research memory becomes an operator: a continuity
architecture that can learn a target, test itself against a baseline, repair
its route, and leave receipts.
